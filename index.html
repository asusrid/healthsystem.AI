Our Service

We provide a comprehensive software solution for continuous monitoring and early detection of cardiovascular diseases. Our service leverages data from blood pressure monitors, wearables, and patient symptoms to ensure timely intervention and improved patient outcomes.

What is ?

Our software continuously monitors patient data to detect early signs of cardiovascular issues. By integrating data from blood pressure monitors, wearables, and patient-reported symptoms, we provide real-time insights and recommendations to healthcare providers, enabling timely and effective intervention.

Technology

PatAInt uses CNNs (Convolutional Neural Networks) units for analyzing and retrieving spatial information from diagnostic images, and LSTM (Long short-term memory) units which are designed to get temporal patterns from data. Patients are our cornerstone, and preserving their privacy is essential following the GDPR.
